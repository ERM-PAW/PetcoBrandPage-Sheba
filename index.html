How SHEBA helps support sustainability

Coral reefs play a vital part in the ocean ecosystem, and they are under threat. Without coral, there’s no fish. That’s why the SHEBA brand has long been committed to supporting the restoration of coral reefs through the SHEBA Hope Grows™ program.

You can play an active role in coral restoration around the world with your own virtual reef. As an owner, you will join the SHEBA Hope Grows™ Collective, enabling you to vote on future projects and get exclusive access to community events. The proceeds of each sale will go towards restoring coral. Your unique virtual coral will eventually be geotagged to a real reef star in the ocean.

In 2021 SHEBA announced their work with Hope Reef, which was the start of a global movement to restore coral reefs around the world. Now, the SHEBA brand is bringing these efforts closer to home by partnering with Kuleana Coral Reefs, an ocean protection organization. Working closely with scientists from Mars, Kuleana is piloting multiple methods of coral restoration, training more teams and restoring more sites in Hawaii.
